About Mirissa Jetliner

Nihal Nandana

Our Mission

At Mirissa Jetliner, we are passionate about providing unforgettable whale watching experiences while prioritizing the conservation of marine life. Operating in Mirissa, we follow international whale watching guidelines (WDCS) to ensure sustainable, eco-friendly tours that respect the ocean's giants.

Why Choose Us?

  • Specialized Vessels: Our boats are designed for whale and dolphin watching, featuring upper deck seating, 1700 HP engines (850 HP per engine), and excellent stability for a comfortable journey.
  • Expert Crew: With over 10 years of experience, our team includes coxswains, lifeguards, a marine engineer, and a zoologist, all approved by the Sri Lanka Tourist Board, Wildlife Department, Coastguard, and Harbour Cooperation.
  • Premium Amenities: Enjoy free tea/coffee, breakfast, fruits, water, juice, Wi-Fi, music, life jackets, insurance, and transport (within 3km) on every tour.
  • Conservation Commitment: We adhere to strict guidelines to minimize disturbance to whales and dolphins, supporting marine conservation efforts.

Our Locations

Experience whale watching across Sri Lanka's prime locations:

  • Mirissa (November–April): Spot blue whales, humpback whales, and dolphins.
  • Trincomalee (May–October): Encounter sperm whales and bottlenose dolphins.
